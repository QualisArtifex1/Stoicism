Week 1 – Gratitude & Self‑Improvement

That which is from fortune is not separated from nature or without an interweaving and involution with the things which are ordered by providence.

Theme: Gratitude & Self‑Improvement

Marcus Aurelius opens his Meditations by thanking mentors and family for instilling virtues such as patience, modesty and love of philosophy【67727104602198†L1473-L1550】. Stoicism emphasises gratitude and the recognition that our character is shaped by our responses to events, not by the events themselves【473929813363279†L200-L224】. To apply this lesson today, reflect on those who have influenced you and commit to continuous self‑improvement. Keep a gratitude journal and remind yourself that you control your judgments and actions, not other people or external circumstances.
