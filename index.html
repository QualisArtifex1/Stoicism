Stoic Principles

  • Only Virtue is Good: character and wisdom are the only true goods; externals like wealth and health are indifferent.
  • Dichotomy of Control: distinguish between what you can control—your thoughts and actions—and what you cannot. Let go of the rest.
  • Live According to Nature: fulfil your role in the universe by cooperating with others and acting rationally.
  • Impermanence: everything changes and passes away; remembering this reduces attachment and anxiety.
